About

Eggenda works to keep you both productive and entertained! We are not just a normal To-do list app, but you can use your accomplishments to gain points and unlock characters, pets, accessories to level up. This gives a sense of reward for the hard work that you have done, helping to motivate you to complete your tasks ASAP!
